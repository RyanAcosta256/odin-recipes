Pork katsudon


This is a simple hearty dish that is easy to make and sometimes seen as a dish that pushes one to success


Indgredients

  • pork cutlet
  • salt and pepper
  • flour(for dusting)
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup panko
  • oil
  • 1/2 cup dashi stock or chicken stock
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons Mirin
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 medium onion(thinly sliced)
  • 2 servings steamed white rice
  • 1 scallion(dropped)
